> Community edition with essential copy research framework.
> Upgrade to RC Engine Pro for VOC mining, Schwartz awareness mapping, objection taxonomies, and competitive copy auditing.

## Purpose

Guide the Copy Research Brief phase. Before writing copy, understand the user's language, awareness level, and decision drivers. This prevents generic, feature-listing copy.

---

## Copy Research Brief Structure

### 1. Voice of Customer (VOC) Extraction
Mine user language from PRD, ICP data, and competitor reviews:
- **Phrases users actually say** about the problem (use their words, not yours)
- **Emotional triggers**: What frustrates them? What excites them?
- **Questions they ask** before buying/signing up

### 2. Awareness Level Assessment
Where is the target user on the awareness spectrum?

| Level | User Knows | Copy Approach |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| **Unaware** | Nothing about the problem | Lead with the problem, not the solution |
| **Problem-Aware** | They have a problem, not the solutions | Agitate the pain, then introduce solution category |
| **Solution-Aware** | Solutions exist, not your product | Differentiate — why yours is better |
| **Product-Aware** | Your product, but hasn't acted | Remove objections, add urgency |
| **Most Aware** | Ready to buy, needs a push | Direct CTA, offer, social proof |

### 3. Jobs-to-Be-Done (JTBD)
Extract the core jobs users are hiring this product to do:
- **Functional**: What task are they completing?
- **Emotional**: How do they want to feel?
- **Social**: How do they want to be perceived?

Format: "When I [situation], I want to [motivation], so I can [expected outcome]."

### 4. Objection Mapping
List the top objections that prevent conversion:
- **Price**: "Is it worth the cost?"
- **Trust**: "Can I trust this company/product?"
- **Effort**: "Is this going to be hard to set up?"
- **Risk**: "What if it doesn't work for me?"
- **Timing**: "Do I need this right now?"

For each: identify the objection, write the counter-copy, and place it strategically.

## Copy Research Brief Output Format

```markdown
# Copy Research Brief — {Project Name}

## VOC Phrases
| Category | User Language | Source |
|----------|--------------|--------|
| Pain | "{exact phrase}" | {ICP/review/interview} |
| Desire | "{exact phrase}" | {source} |
| Question | "{exact phrase}" | {source} |

## Awareness Level
- Primary audience: {level}
- Copy approach: {strategy for that level}

## JTBD Statements
1. When I {situation}, I want to {motivation}, so I can {outcome}.
2. ...

## Top Objections
| Objection | Counter-Copy | Placement |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| {objection} | {how to address it} | {where on the page/flow} |

## Recommended Tone
- {2-3 tone attributes, e.g., "confident but not arrogant", "helpful but not patronizing"}
```
