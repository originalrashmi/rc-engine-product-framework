## Purpose

Provide structured persuasion frameworks for page-level copy. Every page should follow a framework - not just list features.

---

## Page-Level Frameworks

### AIDA (Attention - Interest - Desire - Action)
Best for: Landing pages, product pages, email campaigns

| Stage | Purpose | Copy Element |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **Attention** | Stop the scroll | Headline that addresses the user's problem |
| **Interest** | Build curiosity | Subheadline with specific benefit or stat |
| **Desire** | Create want | Features as benefits, social proof, use cases |
| **Action** | Drive conversion | Clear CTA with low-friction next step |

### PAS (Problem - Agitate - Solution)
Best for: Problem-aware audiences, pain-point marketing

| Stage | Purpose | Copy Element |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **Problem** | Name the pain | "Tired of [specific frustration]?" |
| **Agitate** | Make it urgent | Consequences of not solving it |
| **Solution** | Present the answer | Your product as the relief |

### BAB (Before - After - Bridge)
Best for: Transformation messaging, case studies

| Stage | Purpose | Copy Element |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **Before** | Current painful state | "Right now, you're spending 3 hours on..." |
| **After** | Desired outcome | "Imagine finishing in 15 minutes..." |
| **Bridge** | Your product connects them | "Here's how [product] makes it happen..." |

## CTA Writing Rules

1. **Use action verbs**: "Get started", "Try free", "See pricing" - not "Submit" or "Click here"
2. **Be specific**: "Start my free trial" > "Sign up"
3. **Reduce friction**: "No credit card required", "Cancel anytime"
4. **One primary CTA per section**: Don't compete with yourself
5. **Match awareness level**: Unaware = "Learn more", Product-Aware = "Start free trial"

## Social Proof Patterns

| Type | Format | Best For |
|------|--------|---------|
| **Testimonial** | Quote + name + role + photo | Trust building |
| **Metric** | "10,000+ teams use..." | Scale/popularity |
| **Logo bar** | Customer/partner logos | B2B credibility |
| **Case study** | Before/after with specifics | Complex products |
| **Rating** | Stars + review count | Consumer products |

---

## Headline Formulas

1. **Benefit + Specificity**: "Build landing pages 3x faster"
2. **Problem + Solution**: "Stop losing leads. Start converting."
3. **How-to**: "How [audience] [achieves outcome] without [pain]"
4. **Question**: "Still manually tracking [task]?"
5. **Proof**: "[Number] teams switched from [competitor] this month"

## Framework Selection Guide

| User Awareness | Recommended Framework |
|---------------|----------------------|
| Unaware | PAS (lead with the problem) |
| Problem-Aware | PAS or AIDA (agitate then solve) |
| Solution-Aware | BAB (show transformation) |
| Product-Aware | AIDA (build desire, drive action) |
| Most Aware | Direct CTA with offer/urgency |
