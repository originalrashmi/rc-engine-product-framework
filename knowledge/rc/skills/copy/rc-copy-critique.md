## Conversion Heuristics Checklist

### 1. Clarity (Weight: 30%)

| Check | Question | Pass Criteria |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| **5-second test** | Can a first-time visitor answer "What is this? For whom? What do I do?" | All three answered from above-fold content |
| **One big idea** | Is there a single dominant message above the fold? | One headline, not competing messages |
| **CTA clarity** | Is the primary CTA label literal about what happens next? | "Start free trial" not "Get started" |
| **Jargon check** | Would the ICP understand every word? | Zero unexplained technical terms |
| **Scanability** | Can someone who only reads headings get the message? | Headlines tell a complete story alone |

### 2. Persuasion Framework (Weight: 25%)

| Check | Question | Pass Criteria |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| **Framework flow** | Does the page follow the selected framework in order? | Each section maps to a framework stage |
| **Proof placement** | Is proof placed near CTAs? | Testimonials/metrics within 1 scroll of CTA |
| **Messaging hierarchy** | Does copy follow the defined hierarchy? | Positioning → claims → proof → CTA |

### 3. Voice & Tone (Weight: 20%)

| Check | Question | Pass Criteria |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| **Voice consistency** | Does all copy sound like the same brand? | Swap any section with a competitor's - it should feel wrong |
| **Tone adaptation** | Does tone shift correctly by context? | Marketing enthusiastic, errors calm |
| **DON'Ts respected** | Are brand voice prohibitions followed? | No "just", "simple", "easy" |

### 4. Specificity & Evidence (Weight: 15%)

| Check | Question | Pass Criteria |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| **Quantified claims** | Are claims specific and measurable? | "73% faster" not "significantly faster" |
| **VOC integration** | Does copy use customer's own words? | At least 3 VOC phrases in final copy |
| **Outcome-framed** | Do headlines lead with outcomes? | "Cut reporting time by 73%" not "Automated reporting tool" |

### 5. Microcopy (Weight: 10%)

| Check | Question | Pass Criteria |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| **Button labels** | Are all buttons specific verbs? | Zero "Submit", "Click here", "OK" |
| **Error messages** | Are errors constructive? | Every error tells how to fix it |
| **Empty states** | Are empty states motivating? | Every empty state has a CTA |
