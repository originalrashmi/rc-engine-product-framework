# Product Legal Checklist

Comprehensive checklist used by the Product Legal Auditor to identify
missing legal requirements in user products.

## All Products (Universal Requirements)

- [ ] Terms of Service / Terms of Use
- [ ] Privacy Policy
- [ ] Cookie Policy (if web-based and targeting EU)
- [ ] Copyright notice (footer, about page)
- [ ] Limitation of liability clause
- [ ] Disclaimer of warranties
- [ ] Dispute resolution / arbitration clause
- [ ] Governing law / jurisdiction clause
- [ ] Acceptable use policy (if user-generated content)
- [ ] Intellectual property ownership clause

## Products with User Accounts

- [ ] Account creation consent (explicit agreement to ToS)
- [ ] Password/credential security commitments
- [ ] Account deletion / data erasure process
- [ ] Multi-factor authentication option (recommended for sensitive data)
- [ ] Session management and timeout policy
- [ ] Account recovery process

## Products Collecting User Data

- [ ] Privacy policy covering: what data, why, how long, who sees it
- [ ] Consent mechanism (opt-in for non-essential data)
- [ ] Data retention policy
- [ ] Data deletion / export mechanism
- [ ] Third-party data sharing disclosure
- [ ] Data breach notification process
- [ ] Data processing agreements with vendors

## Products with AI Components

- [ ] AI-generated output disclaimer
- [ ] Accuracy limitation disclosure
- [ ] Human review recommendation for critical decisions
- [ ] Training data provenance disclosure (if applicable)
- [ ] Bias and fairness statement
- [ ] Automated decision-making disclosure (GDPR Art. 22)
- [ ] Opt-out of AI processing option (where applicable)

## Products Handling Payments

- [ ] Refund policy
- [ ] Subscription cancellation process
- [ ] PCI-DSS compliance statement (or use of compliant processor)
- [ ] Payment processor terms reference
- [ ] Pricing transparency (no hidden fees)
- [ ] Auto-renewal disclosure
- [ ] Currency and tax handling disclosure

## Products with User-Generated Content

- [ ] Content ownership / license terms
- [ ] Content moderation policy
- [ ] DMCA / copyright takedown process
- [ ] Prohibited content guidelines
- [ ] User reporting mechanism

## Products Targeting Specific Regions

### EU / EEA
- [ ] GDPR compliance (full checklist above)
- [ ] Cookie consent banner (granular, not pre-checked)
- [ ] Data Protection Officer contact
- [ ] EU representative (if company is outside EU)

### California (US)
- [ ] CCPA disclosures in privacy policy
- [ ] "Do Not Sell or Share" link
- [ ] Consumer request handling process

### Children (Under 13)
- [ ] COPPA compliance (verifiable parental consent)
- [ ] Age gate / verification
- [ ] No behavioral advertising
- [ ] Data minimization

## Products in Regulated Industries

### Healthcare
- [ ] HIPAA compliance program
- [ ] Business Associate Agreements
- [ ] PHI encryption (rest + transit)
- [ ] Access controls and audit logging
- [ ] Breach notification procedures

### Finance / Payments
- [ ] PCI-DSS compliance
- [ ] Anti-money laundering (AML) considerations
- [ ] Know Your Customer (KYC) if applicable
- [ ] Financial data security measures

### Education
- [ ] FERPA compliance (if handling student records)
- [ ] Parental rights disclosure
- [ ] Student data privacy policy
